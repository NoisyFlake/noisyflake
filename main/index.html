Who am I?

I'm 22 years old and I recently started producing electronic music. I live in Germany and work full time as a software developer, so please forgive me if my releases take some time. Expect some rise in quality over time as I'm learning new stuff everyday.

If you'd like to support me, please consider buying one of my tracks as producing music is actually a really expensive hobby; if you can't afford it, tell your friends about me or simply enjoy my music.
