Description

Enables the modern flashlight and camera shortcuts on the lockscreen for all devices. Each button can be enabled separately.

Additionally you can disable 3D Touch, allowing you to just tap the buttons instead. This may not work with other tweaks that change the behavior of those buttons, e.g. Jumper.

Not compatible with HomeGesture (Lite).
