Description

Enables the modern flashlight and camera shortcuts on the lockscreen for all devices. Each button can be enabled separately and moved to any desired position.

Additionally you can disable 3D Touch, allowing you to just tap the buttons instead.

Not compatible with HomeGesture (Lite).
