Description

Color your Control Center

Magma is a tweak that lets you customize almost every color in your control center. Choose your favorite colors from a color picker or enter Hex Codes for the following items:
Connectivity Toggles (WiFi, Bluetooth, ...)
Active Toggle Glyph (Flashlight, Do Not Disturb, AirPlay, ...)
Brightness and Volume sliders
